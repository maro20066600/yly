About YLY Competition

What is YLY?

We are the volunteers of the Ministry of Youth and Sports in the "Youth Leading Youth" (YLY) project, which was launched by Minister of Youth and Sports, Dr. Ashraf Sobhy, since he assumed office in 2018.

The project is spread across all governorates of Egypt and includes a large number of young participants within seven committees: Human Resources (HR), Social Media (SM), Public Relations (PR), Operations (OR), Research and Development (R&D), Media, and Training.

The project has a clear vision aimed at expanding youth participation, with objectives that focus on nurturing young Egyptian talents and showcasing them in a way that reflects positively on society.

What is the YLY Competition?

It is one of the central projects within the YLY initiative, with three successful editions already completed. The competition provides an opportunity for young people from all Egyptian governorates to showcase their talents, skills, and creativity through a variety of competitive events.

Objectives of the competition

  • Providing a suitable environment for young people to highlight their skills and talents.
  • Promoting leadership and innovation among Egyptian youth.
  • Encouraging teamwork and collaboration among participants.
  • Enhancing engagement through social media to showcase youth creativity.
