About YLY Competition

Our Magical Journey

Welcome to the YLY Competition, where we transform education into a magical experience for young minds. Founded in 2023, our competition draws inspiration from the enchanting world of wizardry and magic, while focusing on real-world skills and knowledge.

At YLY Competition, we believe that learning should be an adventure. We've created a unique space where young talents can explore their interests, discover new passions, and develop essential skills in a supportive and imaginative environment.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to inspiring creativity, fostering collaboration, and building confidence in young learners through engaging educational competitions.

  • To create magical learning experiences that ignite curiosity and passion for knowledge
  • To develop critical thinking skills through creative problem-solving challenges
  • To build a supportive community where young talents can connect and grow together
  • To celebrate diverse talents across multiple disciplines and subject areas
  • To prepare young wizards for future academic and career success

Competition Structure

YLY Competition is conducted in two formats: Online and Offline. The offline phase includes on-ground competitions held in different governorates, while the online phase allows participants to submit entries remotely, reaching a wider audience.

Achievements & Impact

Since its launch, YLY Competition has successfully:

  • Discovered and nurtured new talents in various fields.
  • Provided national-level recognition to young achievers.
  • Created a network of future leaders who continue to contribute to their communities.
  • Strengthened digital presence, with thousands engaging with the competition online.

Annual Recognition & Awards

Each year, the top-performing governorates and individuals receive official recognition, awards, and the YLY Competition Trophy. The latest edition saw Suez, Beni Suef, and Alexandria securing the top three positions in the national ranking.
